Rib-eye Steak

Description

Ribeye steak is a cut of beef from the cow's rib primal that's known for its tenderness, juiciness, and rich marbling of fat

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 ounce) ribeye steak
  • 1/2 teaspoon seasoned salt, such as Lawry'sÂ® Seasoned Salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon freshly cracked black pepper, or to taste

Steps

  1. Gather the ingredients.
  2. Season steak evenly, using 1/4 teaspoon seasoned salt and 1/8 teaspoon black pepper on each side. Set aside.
  3. Light charcoal briquettes. Once they are red hot, place steak on the grates. Grill for 4 to 5 minutes. Turn steak over and grill an additional 2 to 3 minutes.
  4. Tent steak with aluminum foil and let rest 4 to 5 minutes before serving.
  5. Serve hot with a dash of butter and Ranch.
